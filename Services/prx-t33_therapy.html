PRX-T33 Therapy

Price: $150

PRX-T33 is light and brief procedure without a need for a rehabilitation period consisting of four sessions. It is the most popular peeling therapy on cosmetology market. PRX-T33 is a dermal stimulation product, first of its kind and consisting of three acids: trichloroacetic acid, kojic acid and hydrogen peroxide. The main purpose of the product is excellent clearance of pigmentations (lightening the skin). It has pronounced lifting effect and improves skin tone. From the very first days of its appearance in the market of peeling agents, PRX-T33 soared to the top: currently clients of beauty salons discover more and more of its benefits, such as its availability all year round (not having to wait for cool weather or reduction in UV activity).

This procedure offers:

  • Pores reduction;
  • Lifting;
  • More even skin tone;
  • Firmer, well-hydrated skin;
  • No need for a rehabilitation period or special skin care (doesn’t cause redness, peeling or skin swelling);
  • Pain-free procedure;
  • Suitable for clients above 15 years of age;
  • Pronounced positive effect after the first session which lasts for a significant period of time;
  • Absence of photosensitivity (clients able to suntan a couple of days after the procedure);
  • Short duration of the procedure (under 15 minutes)
  • Can serve as “going out” procedure, as the skin gains “wow effect” on the day of the treatment and appears groomed and well-rested for many hours after;

For established results this procedure requires 2-6 sessions depending on the skin type and its care needs.

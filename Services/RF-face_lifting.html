What is RF-Face lifting?

Many patients asks their doctors what RF procedure entails despite this treatment being available for a while now and renown for its beneficial effects. More importantly its enjoyed by many clients due its wide availability and affordable cost.

RF-lifting is a revolutionary technology for addressing a number of skin issues. During the treatment session not only there is fat tissue reduction, but also an increase of production of collagen and elastin responsible for firmness and tone of the skin.

Due to RF lifting you can achieve following results:

  • Correction of the facial contour;
  • Smoothing of wrinkles;
  • Elimination of bags under eyes;
  • Improvement of skin tone and firmness;

This rejuvenating treatment is appropriate for all ages. For the younger group of patients it gives an opportunity to get rid of unwanted skin defects, whilst older clients enjoy skin elasticity and firmness whilst eliminating wrinkles and skin imperfections.

All these processes happen pain-free and take less than 20 minutes. More importantly, it will exceed all your expectations!
